Gun-A-Be-Safe



Gun-A-Be-Safe is a mobile application designed and created by 4 University of Washington students in the Interactive Media Design program. With a public health approach, the app will hopefully open up conversations about gun violence and ensure safety in every community. Everyone has a right to safety.

Live Gun Shot Detection

Arduino sensors that detect gun shot sounds are placed in public spaces. By detecting the frequency and wavelength of a noise, a gun shot triggers the sensors to alert your phone. You will always know if you are in a safe environnment with GPS location enabled.

Keep Your Loved Ones Safe

Quickly view if your loved ones are safe from gun violence with "points of interests." Save frequent locations and view its surroundings on the map.
